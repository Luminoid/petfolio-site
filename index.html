🐾What Petfolio does

🐾

Every pet, a profile of their own

  • 8 species: dogs, cats, rabbits, birds, small mammals, fish, reptiles. Fish, reptiles, and "other" use free-text breeds.
  • 477 breeds across 5 species, with AKC / TICA / ARBA groupings and per-species size classes. Generic mixed / domestic entries cover pets without a known pedigree.
  • Profile hub: hero photo, age, weight, and a 3×3 tile grid for health, weight, vet, photos, food, life events, expenses, insurance, and emergency contacts.
  • Unlimited photos per pet, EXIF preserved.
💚

Health tracking, carefully logged

  • Weight — line chart with gaining / losing / stable trend and a multi-pet comparison.
  • Vaccines and parasite prevention (internal / external / combo) — overdue, due-today, and upcoming badges.
  • Medications — plan / log split survives prescription changes; interval-based or as-needed; tap-to-log from the list.
  • Symptoms and baths — free-text entries that join the timeline.
  • Care Journal — unified reverse-chronological timeline with filter chips. Undoable swipe-to-delete.
📅

Calendar and reminders

  • List view (overdue / today / upcoming) and month view.
  • Notifications scheduled up to 7 days ahead, with overdue + today badge counts.
  • Aggregates vaccines, parasite prevention, meds, vet follow-ups, important days, and insurance renewals.
🥣

Resources tab — every household catalog in one place

  • Cross-pet rollup of Expenses, Food, Nutrition, Active Medications, Litter, Bedding, and Vet Contacts.
  • Catalog entries attach to a single pet or live at the household level — household items travel through Family Sharing.
  • Food, nutrition, and litter carry product photos, ratings, prices, and detail screens.
  • Insurance premiums roll into Expenses, prorated by billing cycle and split day-by-day across years.
🏥

Vet visits & insurance

  • Vet visits with PDF and image attachments — QuickLook preview; attach from Files or Photos.
  • App-wide vet directory with copyable values and tap-to-call, email, or open in Maps.
  • Per-pet insurance with renewal dates that surface in the calendar and reminders.
📷

Photos & Live Photos

  • Multiple stills and full Live Photos per pet — paired JPG + MOV, in-app playback, preserved through export and import.
  • Subject-lifted hero avatar — your pet's silhouette overlays the circle and animates with the Live Photo on long-press.
✨

Smart helpers

  • Gemini AI text extraction — photograph a vet record, vaccine label, parasite product, or medication bottle; confirm the extracted dates before they save. Covers all seven event types. Bring your own free API key, stored in the iOS Keychain.
  • Home screen widgets — Upcoming Events and a rotating Pet Photo.
  • Spotlight search — find any pet by name or breed.
💾

Your data, your device

  • Everything stays on your device or iCloud — we don't run servers and never see your records.
  • Full ZIP backup (profile and per-item photos included) with merge or replace on restore. Zip Slip-protected, validate-before-delete.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Share your household

Family Sharing lets everyone who cares for your pets see the same records, reminders, and photos. Invite a partner, housemate, or pet sitter with one tap — and keep the whole household in sync through iCloud.
