Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026 (v1.0.0)

Scope and Acceptance

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") governs the collection, use, disclosure, and retention of information in connection with the mobile application "Petfolio" (the "App"), operated by Chunhou Li ("we," "us," or "our"). By installing, accessing, or using the App, you ("User" or "you") acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Policy. If you do not agree to this Policy, you must not use the App.

Definitions

For the purposes of this Policy:

  • "Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • "Processing" means any operation or set of operations performed on Personal Data (e.g., collection, storage, alteration, disclosure).
  • "Data Controller" means the natural or legal person who determines the purposes and means of Processing.
  • "User Data" means all data entered, generated, or stored by the User within the App, including without limitation pet profiles, photos, health records, care logs, vet visit attachments, inventory, and settings.

Data Controller and Key Principle

The Data Controller in respect of Personal Data Processed in connection with the App is Chunhou Li. Correspondence may be sent to: diveintothefog@gmail.com. A physical address may be requested for legal or regulatory purposes by contacting the Data Controller at the foregoing email.

We do not operate any servers and do not store, retain, or Process any User Data on systems under our control. All User Data remains solely on the User's device or, where the User has enabled iCloud sync, on infrastructure operated by Apple Inc. ("Apple") as part of the iCloud service (under the User's Apple ID). We do not have access to, and do not receive or transmit, User Data.

1. Categories of Data Processed

1.1 Data Provided by the User

In the course of using the App, the User may input or otherwise provide the following categories of data, which are Processed locally on the User's device and, where iCloud sync is enabled, by Apple on behalf of the User:

  • Pet profile data: Name, species, breed, date of birth, gender, weight, feeding notes, and favorite foods or treats.
  • Photographs: Pet photos added to the App, including hero profile images and per-pet photo galleries.
  • Health records: Weight logs; vaccine logs (name, administered date, next-due date, vet information); parasite prevention logs (product, date, dosage, weight at time, optional kind: internal / external / combo); medication plans and dose logs; bath logs (date, products, groomer, cost); symptom logs (date, tags, free-text notes, optional photo); life events and milestones (birthday, adoption, custom milestones); vet visit logs (date, clinic, vet, diagnosis, treatment, cost, follow-up).
  • Photographs & Live Photos: Pet photos may be added as standard images or as Live Photos (paired JPEG + MOV files). When the User selects a Live Photo, both the still and motion components are stored locally on the User's device alongside any preserved EXIF metadata.
  • Attachments: PDF and image files (e.g., vet records, lab results) attached to vet visit logs and stored locally on the User's device.
  • Inventory and household catalogs: Food items, nutrition / supplement items, and litter items (with attributes such as brand or product name, type, rating, price, and optional product photo or product link). Each catalog entry may be associated with a single pet or kept at the household level so that it travels through Family Sharing alongside per-pet items.
  • Vet contact directory: App-wide clinic and vet contact records (name, address, phone, email, notes). The User may copy values or initiate calls, emails, or map directions from the directory; all such actions occur on the User's device using system services.
  • Insurance policies: Per-pet provider, premium, renewal date, billing cycle, and policy notes. Premiums are prorated for expense roll-up locally on the device.
  • Household structure: A "Family" record groups all pets, contacts, and household catalog items; the User may share the Family with other iCloud users via Family Sharing (see Section 3.3).
  • App settings: Weight unit, currency, date format, notification preferences (enabled/disabled, reminder time), default sort and filter options, Gemini AI model preference (if configured), and iCloud sync preference.

1.2 Data Obtained Automatically by the App

The following data may be obtained or derived by the App on the User's device only; none of such data is transmitted to us:

  • Photo metadata: EXIF data (e.g., date taken) or the creation date of the photo asset may be read to populate date fields automatically. Such Processing occurs solely on the User's device.
  • Date and calendar: The device's date and calendar may be used to compute next-due dates, age, countdowns, and reminder schedules. No location or timezone data is transmitted to us.
  • Device information: Apple's App Store and system services may collect information (e.g., iOS version, device model) in accordance with Apple's policies. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking.

1.3 Data Not Collected

We do not collect, and the App is not designed to collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, physical address) except where voluntarily provided by the User when contacting us (e.g., support inquiries).
  • Location data. The App does not request or use location services.
  • Payment or financial information (the App does not process payments).
  • Browsing history or activity outside the App.

2. Purposes and Legal Basis for Processing

2.1 Purposes

User Data is Processed solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the core functionality of the App (pet profiles, health tracking, reminders, calendar, vet visits, food / nutrition / litter inventory, insurance, and life events).
  • To enable optional features such as notifications, iCloud sync, Family Sharing of a household, and AI-powered text extraction from vet records or medication labels via Google's Gemini API (when the User initiates an extraction).
  • Where the User has enabled iCloud sync, to permit storage and synchronization of User Data across the User's devices via Apple's iCloud service. Such Processing is performed by Apple pursuant to the User's Apple ID and Apple's terms and privacy policy; we do not perform or control such Processing.
  • Where the User has shared a household via Family Sharing, to permit authorized participants (as selected by the User) to access and modify the shared household data via Apple's CloudKit sharing service.

Where the User contacts us for support (e.g., by email), we Process only the information contained in or attached to that communication for the purpose of responding to the inquiry.

2.2 Legal Basis (EEA / UK GDPR)

To the extent that Processing of Personal Data occurs in connection with the App, such Processing takes place only on the User's device or on infrastructure operated by Apple (when iCloud sync or Family Sharing is enabled). We do not Process Personal Data on systems under our control. Insofar as the App may be considered to Process Personal Data on the User's device, the legal bases under the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 and the UK GDPR are:

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing is necessary for the performance of the contract between the User and us (provision of the App and its core functionality).
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Where the User explicitly enables optional features such as iCloud sync, Family Sharing, or notifications, Processing is based on the User's consent.

3. Storage and Security

3.1 Location of Data

We do not store User Data. All User Data is held in one or both of the following locations only:

  • User's device: User Data — including pet profiles, photos, health records, attachments, and settings — is stored locally on the User's device using the Core Data framework (with NSPersistentCloudKitContainer). Attachments (PDFs and images) are stored in the App's Documents directory. Such data remains on the device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Apple iCloud (optional): Where the User has enabled iCloud sync, the App uses Core Data's CloudKit integration such that a copy of User Data is also stored on infrastructure operated by Apple (iCloud), associated with the User's Apple ID. The Gemini API key is stored in the iOS Keychain and may be synchronized across the User's devices by Apple via iCloud Keychain if the User has that feature enabled. We do not have access to any of this data.

3.2 iCloud Synchronization

When iCloud sync is enabled, User Data may be stored on the User's device and on Apple's iCloud infrastructure. Such data is:

  • Encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple's iCloud service.
  • Subject to Apple's iCloud Terms and Conditions and Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Accessible only to the User on devices signed in with the User's Apple ID (and, where Family Sharing is enabled, to the authorized participants selected by the User).
  • Not accessible to us or to any third party under our control; we do not operate servers or store User Data.

iCloud sync is optional and may be disabled at any time via the App or the device's system settings. Toggling iCloud sync requires the App to relaunch in order to reconfigure its Core Data stack.

3.3 Family Sharing

The App includes an optional Family Sharing feature that allows the User to share their pet household with other iCloud users (e.g., partner, housemate, or pet sitter):

  • Sharing is performed using Apple's CloudKit sharing service via UICloudSharingController. A CKShare is created for the "Family" record, giving authorized participants access to every pet, photo, and log under that household.
  • In version 1, each User may share at most one household they own and may accept at most one household shared by another User. There is no per-pet or per-log sharing granularity.
  • Concurrent edits by multiple participants are reconciled by CloudKit at property granularity on a last-writer-wins basis; we do not perform custom merge reconciliation.
  • Participants are managed by the User (owner) via the system share sheet. The User may stop sharing, remove participants, or leave a shared household at any time.
  • All sharing data is stored and routed by Apple's CloudKit infrastructure; we do not operate sharing servers, and we do not have access to the shared data.

3.4 Technical and Organizational Measures

Security of User Data is determined by its storage location:

  • On the User's device: data is stored using iOS secure storage mechanisms (Core Data, App Sandbox, and the Keychain for sensitive items such as API keys).
  • When iCloud sync or Family Sharing is enabled: data is encrypted and stored on Apple's iCloud infrastructure.
  • No User Data is transmitted to us or to any system operated by us.

4. Third-Party Services and External Links

4.1 Apple Services

The App integrates with the following services operated by Apple Inc.:

  • App Store: Distribution and updates of the App. Apple may collect information in accordance with its Privacy Policy.
  • iCloud and CloudKit: Optional synchronization of User Data and Family Sharing of a pet household. When enabled, User Data is stored on the User's device and on Apple's iCloud infrastructure; we do not store such data.
  • Push Notifications: Delivery of care reminders and CloudKit silent pushes used to notify the App of remote changes. Notification content is Processed by Apple's notification services.
  • Spotlight Search: Pet names, breeds, and species are indexed locally in the device's Spotlight search index (via CoreSpotlight) for quick discovery. Indexing occurs on device only; no index entries or queries are transmitted to us.

Apple's Privacy Policy is available at: https://www.apple.com/privacy/. Users are encouraged to review it.

4.2 Google Gemini API

The App offers an optional AI-powered feature that extracts structured data (e.g., product names, dosage, administered dates) from photographs of vet records, vaccine labels, or medication bottles using Google's Gemini API. This feature is off by default and is available only when the User has entered their own Gemini API key in the App's settings. When the User initiates an extraction:

  • Data sent to Google: The photograph selected by the User (compressed JPEG) and the User's language preference. No pet identification data, contact details, account information, or GPS coordinates are included.
  • Processing by Google: Google processes the request and returns a structured extraction result. Google's processing is subject to Google's Gemini API Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Preview and confirmation: The extraction result is presented to the User for review. Nothing is saved to the User's records until the User confirms.
  • Results stored locally: Confirmed data is stored on the User's device and, where iCloud sync is enabled, on Apple's iCloud infrastructure. We do not store or have access to these results.
  • User-initiated only: No data is sent to Google unless the User explicitly triggers an extraction. The User's Gemini API key is stored in the iOS Keychain.

4.3 Export and Import

The App permits the User to export User Data (pets, health records, photos including Live Photo motion components, household catalogs, attachments, and settings) to a ZIP file on the User's device or to the Files app, and to import from a previously exported ZIP (replace all or merge). Export and import operations are performed solely on the User's device or in locations selected by the User. Exports include a manifest, a data JSON file, a settings JSON file, the photo gallery (still images, paired Live Photo .mov files, and per-item product photos), and vet visit attachments. We do not receive, store, or Process exported or imported data.

4.4 External Links and On-Device Reference Data

The App may contain links to third-party resources for general reference. Accessing such links takes the User outside the App; the privacy practices and terms of those third parties apply. The App also includes built-in reference data (e.g., breed databases for dogs, cats, rabbits, birds, and small mammals) stored locally on the device; such data is not used to identify the User.

4.5 Widgets and Deep Links

The App provides home screen widgets ("Upcoming Events" and "Pet Photo") that display information from the User's records. Widget data is written to a shared App Group container on the User's device and is not transmitted to us. The App also supports petfolio:// URL scheme deep links for navigation to specific pets, calendar dates, and views; deep link activity occurs locally on the device.

  • With authorized household participants: When the User shares a Family household, the participants chosen by the User gain access via Apple's CloudKit sharing service. We do not act as an intermediary.

11.1 Retention

We do not retain User Data, as we do not store it. User Data remains solely on the User's device and, where iCloud sync is enabled, on Apple's iCloud infrastructure. Retention is determined by the User: data is removed from the device when deleted in the App or when the App is uninstalled; data on Apple's infrastructure remains until the User deletes it via Apple ID settings.

11.2 Automated Decision-Making

Features such as next-due date calculations, weight trend analysis, and reminder scheduling are based on User-provided data and the device date and are performed locally on the device; they do not constitute such automated decision-making.

11.3 Personal Data Breach

  • Notify the competent supervisory authority without undue delay and, where feasible, within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach (as required under the GDPR for EEA/UK).

  • Communicate the nature of the breach and the measures taken or proposed to address it.

We do not operate servers or store User Data. All User Data remains on the User's device or on Apple's iCloud infrastructure (when sync or Family Sharing is enabled). We therefore do not hold User Data that could be subject to a breach of our systems; the foregoing obligations apply only to the extent we Process or hold Personal Data.
